Events at SRM AP

Aloha (Freshers' Day)

SRM University-AP, Andhra Pradesh presents Aloha, a virtual welcome to the freshers on this erudite journey. Young students to attend the classes and enjoy the conducive campus of SRM University-AP, an array of performances, contests, and fun awaits them on February . Students are encouraged to unleash their talent in the gamut of competitions hosted by the seniors to celebrate the beginning of the journey of their juniors at SRM University-AP.

Paridhan (Ethnic Day)

Paaridhan: Weaving a Tapestry of Culture and Ethnicity! This was a reality at SRM University-AP on May 01, 2024, as the university transformed into a vibrant hub of cultural pride and unity as it celebrated “Paaridhaan”. Students dazzled the campus with an array of performances, fashion walks, and activities, each showcasing the rich tapestry of our diverse backgrounds. The campus was engulfed in a colourful hue as students attended the celebration adorned in traditional ethnic wear.

Infinitus (Cultural Fest)

INFINITUS- a national level techno-cultural fest comes to a grand close. Min of 2 days fest highlighted SRM University-AP's commitment to fostering creativity, talent, inclusivity, and technological innovation where students showcased their talents, competed, learned, and thrived in an environment that celebrates diversity, unity, and boundless possibilities.

Udgam (Sports Fest)

Udgam is the annual sports festival at SRM University-AP, Andhra Pradesh. The 2024 edition of Udgam was held from February to March. The event celebrated the values of sportsmanship, teamwork, integrity, and learning from every game
